What do we have for you?
Dive in C Trivia is a rich set of multiple choice questions on C programming language and its aspects. The questions are focussed to enrich the understanding of C language of the reader. This trivia will cover all areas of C language namely arrays, pointers, strings, etc. These topics are chosen from The C Programming Language by Kernighan & Ritchie, the most authoritative book on C.
Dive in C Trivia - Highlights
  • Standard Multiple Choice Questions & Answers in C with explanations
  • Lots of MCQs on various topics with C code/programming snippet
  • Topic-wise sorting of multiple choice questions.
  • All C codes are compiled and tested on x86-32 bit Linux systems
Who should solve these?
  • The one who wants to sharpen his or her C programming language skills.
  • The one who is preparing for aptitude test in C.
  • The onw who is preparing for interviews.
  • The one who is preparing for entrance examinations like GATE and GRE.
